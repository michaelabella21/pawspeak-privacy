PawSpeak

Dog Mood Analyzer — by NerdVine.ph

Last updated: April 3, 2026

PawSpeak is developed and operated by NerdVine.ph. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the PawSpeak mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

PawSpeak collects the following types of information:

  • Dog profiles — name, breed, age, and personality traits you enter manually. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Mood logs — bark recordings, behavior signals, mood classifications, and confidence scores. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Health alerts — pattern anomaly records generated automatically based on your dog's mood history. Stored locally on your device only.
  • App preferences — language selection and feature settings. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Location data — used only when you access the Vet Finder feature to find nearby veterinary clinics. Location is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

2. Optional Data Sharing

If you enable the "Help improve PawSpeak" toggle in Settings, anonymized bark audio recordings and their associated mood labels may be uploaded to our servers to improve the AI model. This is strictly opt-in and off by default.

  • No personally identifiable information is attached to contributed recordings
  • Dog names, your name, or account information are never included
  • You can withdraw consent at any time by turning off the toggle in Settings

3. Audio Recording

PawSpeak requests microphone permission to record your dog's vocalizations. Audio is recorded only when you actively press and hold the record button. All audio processing happens on your device — audio files are never transmitted to our servers unless you have explicitly opted in to data contribution as described above.

4. Third-Party Services

PawSpeak uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — used to deliver push notifications such as health alerts and app update notices. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • PostHog — used for anonymous usage analytics (screen views, feature usage). No personal data is collected. No user identifiers are transmitted.
  • OpenStreetMap / Overpass API — used in the Vet Finder feature to retrieve nearby veterinary clinic data. Your approximate location is sent to the Overpass API only when you use this feature. No account or personal data is transmitted.

5. Data Storage & Security

All personal data — dog profiles, mood logs, health alerts, and settings — is stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly opt in to data contribution. We do not operate user accounts and do not store personal data on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

PawSpeak is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Health Disclaimer

PawSpeak is a behavioral pattern detection tool only. It is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Health alerts generated by PawSpeak are observations, not medical diagnoses. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for any health concerns about your pet.

8. Your Rights

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can:

  • Delete any dog profile, mood log, or health alert directly within the app
  • Uninstall PawSpeak to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt out of data contribution at any time via Settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of PawSpeak after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
